# 🏛️ $GOV: The Meritocratic Equity Token
**Classification:** Non-Transferable Governance / Reputation Token
**Issuance:** Proof-of-Contribution (PoC) Only

## 1. The Core Philosophy
In terrestrial systems, capital buys influence (1 Dollar = 1 Vote). This leads to oligarchy. In Bitopia, influence cannot be bought; it must be earned. `$GOV` represents a Citizen's verifiable contribution to the Network State. It is soul-bound (non-transferable) to prevent whales from purchasing political power.

## 2. Minting Logic (Proof-of-Contribution)
`$GOV` is minted automatically by the dasOS Oracles when a Citizen performs a verifiable action that benefits the State.

**Eligible Contributions & Weighting:**
* **Engineering/Code Commits:** High weight (e.g., merging core logic).
* **Judicial Arbitration:** Medium weight (serving as a fair node in dispute resolution).
* **Mathematical/Scientific Proofs:** High weight (optimizing thermodynamic capture).
* **Network Security/Debugging:** Medium weight (identifying vulnerabilities).

## 3. The Madison Protocol: Quadratic Voting
To protect the minority from the tyranny of the majority (or early adopters), all systemic governance votes utilizing `$GOV` are processed through a Quadratic Voting algorithm.

**The Mathematical Constraint:**
The voting power ($V$) of a Citizen is equal to the square root of their staked `$GOV` tokens ($S$).

$$V = \sqrt{S}$$

*Example:* * Citizen A has 1 `$GOV` = 1 Vote.
* Citizen B has 100 `$GOV` = 10 Votes.
*(Citizen B contributed 100x more, but only wields 10x the influence, forcing them to build consensus rather than dictating terms).*

## 4. Slashing Conditions
If a Citizen acts maliciously (e.g., attempting a 51% attack, submitting fraudulent Oracle data), the Decentralized Judicial Nodes can execute a "Slash" command, burning a percentage of their `$GOV` and severely reducing their systemic influence.
