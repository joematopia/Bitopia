# 🏛️ THE LEGISLATIVE & BUDGETARY PROTOCOL
**Classification:** Standard Governance & Treasury Allocation  
**Mechanism:** Quadratic Voting & Milestone-Based Smart Contracts

---

## I. THE CORE PHILOSOPHY
While the Bitopia Codex is intentionally difficult to change (requiring Article V), standard legislation and treasury budgets must remain agile. The Network State funds engineering, space infrastructure, and judicial nodes through a transparent, mathematically verifiable pipeline. We reject "blank checks"; all funding is streamed based on cryptographic proof of work.

---

## II. THE LEGISLATIVE PIPELINE (STANDARD LAWS)
For proposing operational rules, integrating new Oracles, or adjusting standard network parameters:

### A. Phase I: The Draft & Stake
A Citizen submits a **Bitopia Improvement Proposal (BIP)**. They must stake **500 $GOV** reputation to prevent network spam.

### B. Phase II: The Epoch (Debate)
The proposal enters a mandatory 7-day terrestrial debate period for public audit and discussion.

### C. Phase III: The Vote
Active Citizens cast their votes utilizing the **$GOV** reputation token.
* **Mechanism:** Quadratic Voting ($V = \sqrt{S}$).
* **Quorum:** At least 15% of the total circulating $GOV supply must participate for the vote to be valid.
* **Threshold:** Requires a simple majority (>50% of the quadratic weight) to pass.

### D. Phase IV: Execution
If passed, **dasOS** automatically integrates the logic. The staker's 500 $GOV is returned with a 5% merit reward. If failed, the stake is returned without reward.

## III. THE BUDGETARY PROCESS (TREASURY ALLOCATION)
Bitopia utilizes **Milestone-Based Streaming** for all treasury deployments.

### A. The Proposal
A contractor (e.g., a CubeSat manufacturing team) requests a specific sum of **$SOV** to build infrastructure.

### B. The Smart Escrow (Streaming)
If approved by the vote, the $SOV is locked in a time-release smart contract, divided into tranches (e.g., 20% upfront, 40% upon successful launch, 40% upon first telemetry).

### C. The Axiom Proof-of-Work Gate
To unlock subsequent tranches, the contractor must submit cryptographic proof. For hardware, this requires telemetry signed by an **Axiom Hardware Enclave**. The Judicial Nodes verify the proof, and the contract automatically releases the funds.

---

## IV. THE EMERGENCY VETO (THE CIRCUIT BREAKER)
If a critical vulnerability is discovered in a passed budget before execution, a "Circuit Breaker" can be triggered.

### A. The Bitaris Swarm Veto
The **Axiom-4 orbital nodes** or a rapid 24-hour flash-vote of the Citizenry can trigger an `Emergency_Halt`.

### B. Resolution
This freezes the smart contract and refunds the treasury to the Sovereign Trust, protecting the State from "Zero-Day" governance exploits.
